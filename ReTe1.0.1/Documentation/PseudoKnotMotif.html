PseudoKnotMotif


This is a Motif subclass to identify possible pseudoknots. You will not understand this unless you are acquainted with pseudoknots.

Its identifying string is 'PKN'.

Pseudoknots are assumed to have the following structure (lengths in bases):

Stem 1          (5-6)
Loop 1           (1-4)
Stem 2          (5-8)
Unpaired        (0-1)
Stem 1 compl (5-6)
Loop 2           (7-13)
Stem 2 compl (5-8)

Stem 1 is allowed to contain at most two a or t (total in both branches). Loop 2 must contain at least one a. These requirements are on the lax side and may be adjusted in a PseudoKnotMotifsdefault parameter in Configuration.txt using the keys 'Stem1MaxAT' and 'Loop2MinA'.

A temporary score is calculated as 2 times the summed stem lengths, minus 1 for each gt pair in the stems. The final score is the maximal score times the temporary score divided by 28.

Please note that the flexibity is such that a good pseodoknot structure may give several hits by this Motif, in slightly different positions.
