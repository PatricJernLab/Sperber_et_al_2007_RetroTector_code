MakeSubGenes

This Executor in plugins is part of the package to make new Databases.  It constructs a putative SubGenes.txt file (SubGenesCandidate.txt) from a script (typically an edited version of the MakeSubGenesScript.txt file generated by DredgeAlignments) with a parameter something like:

 MA           ?            ?            0<0          428<2006     ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?
 CA           ?            ?            ?            0<0          173<578      ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?
 NC           ?            ?            ?            ?            0<0          255<986      86<1305      ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?
 DU           ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            0<0          189<319      ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?
 Prot         ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            0<0          346<1707     ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?
 RT           ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            0<0          889<2250     988<2753     ?            ?            ?            ?
 DL           ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            0<0          133<503      ?            ?            ?            ?
 IN           ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            0<0          1074<4054    ?            ?            ?
 SU           ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            0<0          135<668      ?            ?
 TM           ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            0<0          271<2092     ?
 PPT          ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            0<0          50<1600
 3LTR         ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            ?            0<0

The diagonal and diagonal + 1 must be filled. Other positions higher up may also be filled.

There are no other parameters, and no point in running interactively.

The Database subdirectory where this is done must first be selected
as working directory.
