RetroTector

 

RMotif


This is an OrdinaryMotif. Instances of this class evaluate the likelihood that an R part of LTR has its hotspot (aaTaaa) at target position, and its extent.

Its identifying string is 'R' .

R end is put at the position within 10-30 positions after the hotspot with highest value for REndModifier.

R start is put at a position 30-300 positions before R end that best fulfills the conditions:

    1-40 positions after a TATAA or CATAAA
    1-100 positions before a hit by the Inr consensus (TC)(TC)AN(TA)(TC)(TC)

These criteria do not seem satisfactory and are under revision.

Raw score is calculated as the product of the REndModifier value at the end and the number of condition fulfilments at the start.
