ShowPseudoKnot


This Executor is a utility to show the internal structure of a presumed pseudoknot.

There are no parameters.

ShowPseudoKnot should be started from the Execute menu of the RetroTector window. A window will the appear, into which a short DNA segment may be pasted. Clicking 'Search in primary strand' will then start a search for a hit by PseudoKnotMotif in this segment. If one is found, its structure will be shown in the text window as shown by this example:

    ShowPseudoKnot output for
ggggccagcctcaggccccacaacaaactggggc
STEM1ASTART 0
STEM1AEND 4
STEM2ASTART 7
STEM2AEND 13
STEM1BSTART 14
STEM1BEND 18
STEM2BSTART 27
STEM2BEND 33
ggggc ca gcctcag
  S1  L1  S2
ccccg    cggggtc

  acaacaaa
    L2


where the second line shows the DNA corresponding to the knot. The following eight lines show the positions in the original sequence of key features in the knot.

Of the last six lines, the first shows the primary strands of stem1, loop1, stem 2 and possibly an intevening base. The third shows the secondary strands (to be read backwards, as it were) of stem1 and stem2. The fifth shows loop 2.
