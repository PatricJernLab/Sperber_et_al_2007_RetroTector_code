Alignment files


There are at present 36 of these parameter files in Database, one for each combination of Gene names (Gag, Pro, Pol, Env) and virus genus symbols. They are named accordingly. They are used by ORFID as templates for puteins.

The essential part of an alignment file is the multiparameter 'Alignment'. It is mainly a standard Clustal alignment, but there is a line, the 'MotifGroup anchor line' or occasionally two lines (see CenvAlignment.txt for an example) added above the alignment lines, with anchor groups of the form <|MotifGroup name|>. These indicate (with the '<') where Motifs in that MotifGroup are located in the alignment.

If you edit alignment files, please note that the MotfiGroup anchors must not extend beyond the alignment lines and may therefore have to be broken over two lines. The RT3 anchor in EpolAlignment.txt provides an example.

There may also be an optional multiparameter 'Suspect' . It is used by DredgeAlignments to indicate that parts of the alignment may not be suitable for calculation of acceptable distances between MotifGroups, and by ORFID to indicate that they may not be suitable patterns for start or end of puteins. Each line in it consists of three contiguous parts:
  • The title of a line in the alignment.
  • <|MotifGroup name|>, or <START>
  • <|MotifGroup name|>, or <END>
The region of the line with the title in question between (inclusive) the points defined by the two latter parts, will not be used by DredgeAlignment or ORFID.
