LTRMotif


This is a Motif which puts information about LTRs from LTRID at the disposal of the fragment threading procedure in RetroVID.

An LTRMotif does not search in the DNA; this has already been done by LTRID, and what is required is the output from LTRID, normally in the form of a RetroVID script.

For each LTR pair recognized by LTRID, there will be three entries in the RetroVID script, one describing the pair as a whole, and one each for the two paired LTR candidates. From the two latter, two LTRMotif hits are formed, each containing a reference to the other. Their scores will be calculated by multiplying the MAXSCORE of the LTRMotif with the sum of the score factors calculated by LTRID for the two LTR candidates.
