SplitAcidMotif


This is an OrdinaryMotif, evaluating a combination of several AcidMotifs.

Its identifying string is 'SPA'.

The properties of a particular motif are specified in the MOTIFSTRING field in Motifs.txt. It should contain a series of alternating strings such as required by AcidMotif in MOTIFSTRING, and range specifiers of the acid type, enclosed in parentheses. It must also contain an '!', which marks the hotspot position. This is an example of a valid MOTIFSTRING:
L(6)L(6)L(1-5)!C(2-15)C
The acid strings are raw scored as in AcidMotif, and the final raw score at a target position as the highest sum of such scores obtainable within the distance constraints. The distances refer to the gaps between the fragments, not their starts.

If there are frame shifts within a hit by this Motif, its acid sequence may not be shown correctly.

The inner working of this Motif are complex and may well contain bugs.
